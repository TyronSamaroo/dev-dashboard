Water-Cut Guardrail

Do not buy OTC diuretics or water pills for this cleanup. The scale drop is temporary dehydration/electrolyte disruption, not better fat loss, and it stacks badly with low calories, low carbs, StairMaster, caffeine, and recent RHR/HRV stress.

Sauna is not a weight-loss lever here. If used at all, keep it to 10-15 minutes for relaxation, never immediately after a hard cardio/lift session, and stop for dizziness, cramps, headache, nausea, unusual heat, or high heart-rate feeling.

Stop / Pullback Triggers

  • Dizziness, faintness, chest pain, confusion, or palpitations.
  • Headache/cramps not fixed by water + sodium.
  • Sleep below 5.5 hours or badly fragmented.
  • Resting HR stays much higher than normal.
  • Lift performance drops more than about 15%.
  • Binge/rebound thoughts start building.
Pullback: add 150-250 calories and reduce cardio by 10-15 minutes.
