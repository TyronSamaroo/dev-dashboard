Which surface should I use?

Use one source for the job at hand instead of bouncing between five messy views.

  • Daily decisionsUse Daily Master and Analysis. These answer what is off, whether the plan is still working, and what lever to adjust next.
  • Filtering/sortingUse the native Google Sheet or Data Ledger. The sheet is better for quick manual row edits; the site ledger is better for reading training/cardio context.
  • Workout/cardio reviewUse Data Ledger → Cardio Sessions for current cleanup details, then Analysis → Cardio trend for StairMaster vs walking reads.
  • Weekly meal reviewUse the Coach Report for the polished week-by-week meal-pattern view you already liked; use Prep Runway for current-day decisions.
  • Backup/exportUse the downloadable workbook only as a portable backup. Upload it to Google Drive and choose “Open with Google Sheets” if you ever need a fresh native copy.
